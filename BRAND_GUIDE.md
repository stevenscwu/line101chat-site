# BRAND GUIDE

## Positioning

LINE101 Studio is a premium Taiwan-based digital studio building:

- high-end websites
- grounded RAG AI assistants
- custom web applications

The brand should feel like a serious boutique studio with scalable capability, not a solo freelancer profile.

## Voice and Tone

- Premium
- Concise
- Modern
- Calm
- Technically capable
- Trustworthy
- International

Avoid:

- loud marketing language
- hype-heavy startup clichés
- cheap pricing framing

## Visual Principles

- Generous spacing and clean hierarchy
- Strong typography with restrained weight changes
- Minimal color palette with subtle gradient accents
- Alternating light/dark section rhythm
- Rounded cards and polished borders/shadows
- Motion should be subtle and purposeful

## UI Direction

- Keep layouts cinematic but structured
- Prioritize readability and intentional whitespace
- Preserve a premium first-screen impression on homepage
- Maintain strong mobile adaptation

## Apple-Inspired, Not Apple-Copied

Allowed:

- clear section-led narrative flow
- minimal surfaces and precise typography
- cinematic hero composition

Not allowed:

- copying Apple branding assets
- mimicking Apple-specific wording
- reproducing Apple layouts 1:1

## Bilingual Rules

- Default locale is `zh-TW`
- English must be fully polished, not secondary-quality
- Do not mix languages inside the same localized experience
- Keep terminology consistent across pages

## Content Style Guardrails

- Keep paragraphs short
- Lead with clarity over adjectives
- Show capability through structure and precision
- Mark non-client examples clearly as concepts/demos/explorations
