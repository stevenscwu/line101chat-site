## 10 Post Topics

### 1. 開課時間一直被問，代表 FAQ 沒有進到 LINE

Pain: staff repeatedly answer schedule questions.  
Demo angle: show one LINE question and a source-grounded answer.

### 2. 家長問費用，不一定要立刻真人回

Pain: pricing questions consume sales time but often have standard boundaries.  
Demo angle: answer basic fee range, then hand off for discounts or custom cases.

### 3. 試聽流程最適合做第一個 LINE AI Pilot

Pain: trial-class questions are frequent, low-risk, and easy to document.  
Demo angle: show flow from question to lead capture.

### 4. 退費和補課不能亂答，所以更需要轉真人規則

Pain: high-risk policy questions need boundaries.  
Demo angle: bot answers general policy and routes exceptions to staff.

### 5. 招生 FAQ 不用 300 頁，先用 20-30 頁就能測試

Pain: buyers think AI projects require huge preparation.  
Demo angle: small Pilot scope and 30 golden questions.

### 6. 如果你的櫃台每天複製貼上同樣回答，這就是 Pilot 題目

Pain: repeated manual work.  
Demo angle: before/after screenshot of LINE repeated Q&A.

### 7. LINE OA 自動回覆和 LINE AI 知識助理差在哪？

Pain: buyers may think LINE OA basic features are enough.  
Demo angle: explain fixed keyword reply vs document-grounded answer with source and handoff.

### 8. 課程顧問不該被 AI 取代，應該被 AI 篩出更好的名單

Pain: fear of replacing staff.  
Demo angle: bot answers standard questions and collects serious leads.

### 9. 做 AI 前，先問：你有沒有 30 題真實問題？

Pain: weak document readiness.  
Demo angle: show the 30-question acceptance test as the buyer-friendly standard.

### 10. 第一個 Pilot 的成功標準：30 題裡 24 題能根據文件回答
