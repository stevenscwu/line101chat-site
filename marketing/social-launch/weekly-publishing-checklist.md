# Weekly Publishing Checklist

Use this checklist to publish consistently without sounding repetitive or overpromotional.

## Monday: Pick The Weekly Theme

- Choose one pain point:
  - 客服一直重複回答
  - 公司文件找不到
  - 新人訓練成本高
  - LINE 訊息太多
  - 客戶晚上也想得到答案
- Choose one use case:
  - 學校招生
  - 企業客服
  - 內部 SOP
  - 觀光旅遊
  - HR / 新人訓練
- Choose one CTA:
  - line101chat.com
  - line101chat.com/case-studies
  - line101chat.com/demo/ifirst-rag
  - line101chat.com/line

## Tuesday: Prepare Visuals

- Select one presenter/avatar image from `/public/presenter`.
- Create one feed image or carousel cover.
- Create one LINE demo screenshot or QR placeholder if no live QR is ready.
- Check that unreleased demos are labeled「demo concept」or「under development」.
- Avoid fake logos, fake testimonials, or fake customer claims.

## Wednesday: Publish Main Post

- Publish one Facebook or LinkedIn post.
- Lead with a practical business pain point.
- Mention「台灣在地 LINE AI 知識助理」once.
- Explain the value in business terms.
- End with one clear CTA.

## Thursday: Repurpose To Short Caption

- Convert the main post into one Threads/Instagram caption.
- Shorten the first line into a clear hook.
- Add 3-5 hashtags from `hashtag-bank.md`.
- Keep the caption useful and calm.

## Friday: Publish Demo-Oriented Content

- Record or mock up a short demo:
  - one question from FAQ
  - one answer in LINE
  - one business takeaway
- Keep video under 60 seconds for Shorts/Reels/TikTok.
- Use a CTA to line101chat.com or a specific demo page.

## End Of Week: Review

- Record which posts were published.
- Note which topic received the most clicks, comments, saves, or replies.
- Collect real questions from comments or DMs.
- Add good questions to future FAQ/demo scripts.
- Decide next week's theme based on actual interest.

## Brand Consistency Check

- Traditional Chinese first.
- Professional, practical, Taiwan SME friendly.
- No fake AI hype.
- No fake customer claims.
- No paid-client claims unless already shown on the website.
- Avoid heavy technical terms unless the post is for technical readers.
- Prefer「先做小型 PoC」over big transformation promises.
- Always keep the next step simple: prepare one FAQ, PDF, or SOP document.

## Fast Weekly Output Target

- 1 Facebook post
- 1 LinkedIn post
- 1 Threads/Instagram caption
- 1 short video script or demo clip
- 1 reusable visual or carousel
