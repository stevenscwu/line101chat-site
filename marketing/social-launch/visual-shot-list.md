## Visual Rules

- Use LINE-style chat mockups only as illustrative demos.
- Mark unreleased concepts as「demo concept」or「under development」.
- Do not use fake customer logos, fake testimonial screenshots, or fake paid-client labels.
- Keep text large enough for mobile feeds.
- Prefer practical screenshots, checklists, and process visuals over futuristic AI imagery.
