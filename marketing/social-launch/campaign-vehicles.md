## Launch Content Vehicles

### Pinned Launch Post

Goal: explain what LINE101Chat is and invite people to test a demo.

Message:
「LINE101Chat 是台灣在地 LINE AI 知識助理，協助企業把 FAQ、SOP、招生資料與服務文件變成可在 LINE 中即時回答的 AI 助理。第一步可以很小：一份文件、一個場景、一個 demo。」

CTA:
「查看 demo 或預約討論：https://line101chat.com/start」

### Demo Proof Post

Goal: show a practical LINE chatbot interaction without claiming paid customer results.

Message:
「不是每個問題都需要真人即時回答。營業時間、服務流程、文件查詢、報名方式，可以先用 LINE chatbot demo 測試。」

CTA:
「查看 LINE chatbot demo：https://line101chat.com/demo/ifirst-rag」

### PoC Offer Post

Goal: reduce buyer hesitation.

Message:
「AI 導入不用一開始就很大。先準備一份 FAQ、PDF 或 SOP，我們用小型 PoC 協助你評估 LINE AI 知識助理是否適合。」

CTA:
「預約討論：https://line101chat.com/book-demo」

## Comment And Inbox Routing

When someone comments or sends a DM:

1. Thank them briefly.
2. Ask what scenario they want to test:
   - 客服 FAQ
   - SOP 文件
   - 招生資料
   - 旅遊服務
   - HR 新人訓練
3. Ask whether they can prepare one FAQ, PDF, or SOP.
4. Send them to:
   - https://line101chat.com/book-demo
   - https://line101chat.com/demo/ifirst-rag

Use `dm-response-templates.md` for ready replies.

## Do Not Use

- Fake client logos
- Fake testimonial screenshots
- "Guaranteed automation" claims
- "Replace your whole customer service team" language
- Paid-client claims unless already shown on the website
- Heavy technical terms in public launch posts
