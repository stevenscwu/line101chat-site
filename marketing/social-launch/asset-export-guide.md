## Export Notes

- Export profile images as PNG when possible.
- Keep the presenter/avatar visible after circular cropping.
- Before uploading banners, preview on desktop and mobile because platforms crop banners differently.
- Replace QR placeholders with actual QR images only after the demo link is confirmed.
- Keep public CTAs focused on website and demo links, not the dedicated phone number.

## Brand Checks Before Upload

- Traditional Chinese first.
- Main phrase appears clearly: 台灣在地 LINE AI 知識助理.
- The visual does not imply fake clients, fake testimonials, or paid customer results.
- The CTA is specific: line101chat.com, demo, case studies, or book demo.
- Unreleased concepts are labeled as demo concept or under development.

## Quick Export Workflow

1. Open the SVG in a browser or design tool.
2. Confirm presenter image path renders correctly from `/public/presenter`.
3. Export as PNG.
4. Upload to the matching platform.
5. Check the final crop in both desktop and mobile views.
