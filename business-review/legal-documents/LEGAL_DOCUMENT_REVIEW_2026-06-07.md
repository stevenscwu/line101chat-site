## Review Findings

### Covered

- Customer confidentiality before receiving non-public documents.
- Master service relationship and SOW ordering.
- Pilot scope, fee, timeline, deliverables, exclusions, and acceptance criteria.
- Data processing, PDPA-style notice support, subprocessors, international transfer, retention, deletion, and incident notification.
- AI limitations, unacceptable uses, regulated/high-risk question handoff, and no 100% accuracy claims.
- Pilot support, maintenance support, SLA exclusions, third-party dependency exclusions.
- Proposal validity, payment, tax/invoice reminder, change requests, cancellation, and no outcome guarantees.
- Electronic document/signature consent language.
- Taiwan governing law and Taipei District Court first-instance jurisdiction, subject to mandatory law review.

### Deliberately Not Finalized

- Exact legal contracting entity.
- Company name, uniform invoice number, registered address, responsible person.
- Tax and invoice wording.
- Consumer-facing refund/cooling-off terms if selling to individual consumers.
- Industry-specific rules for cram schools, universities, healthcare, finance, immigration, government procurement, or minors.
- Final liability cap and enforceability of standard terms.
- Final data processor/subprocessor list and cross-border transfer disclosures.
- Final SLA commitments for production hosting.

## Required Human Review Before Signing

1. Confirm whether LINE101Chat is signing as a company, sole proprietor, natural person, or another legal entity.
2. Confirm whether the customer is B2B, school, government, cram school, or individual consumer.
3. Confirm whether any personal data, minor data, student data, payment data, health data, or official decision data will be processed.
4. Confirm all third-party services actually used in the customer deployment.
5. Confirm tax, invoice, payment, refund, and accounting treatment.
6. Confirm jurisdiction, liability cap, warranty exclusions, and electronic signature method.
7. Confirm whether additional government, education, cram-school, consumer, or sector-specific terms are required.

## Auditor Recommendation

Use these documents for controlled pre-sales and Pilot preparation, but do not send them as final signature-ready agreements without a Taiwan lawyer/accountant review. The most important immediate blocker remains the legal signing entity.
